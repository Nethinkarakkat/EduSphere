## Getting Started

### Account Registration

1. Navigate to the EduSphere login page
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Fill in your information:
   - **Name:** Full name
   - **Email:** Institutional email address
   - **Password:** Strong password (minimum 6 characters)
   - **Confirm Password:** Re-enter password
   - **Role:** Select "Student"
4. Click **"Sign Up"**

### Account Approval

Student accounts are automatically approved upon registration. You can log in immediately after signing up.

### First-Time Setup

1. Complete your profile:
   - Add your registration number
   - Specify your program/course
   - Add your section/batch
   - Add your date of birth (optional)
   - Add your phone number (optional)
2. Upload a profile picture (optional)
3. Change your password if needed

### Logging In

1. Navigate to the EduSphere login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Select "Student" as your role
4. Click **"Login"**

## Classroom Management

### Joining a Classroom

1. Navigate to **Classrooms** from the dashboard
2. Click **"Join Classroom"**
3. Enter the classroom code provided by your teacher
4. Click **"Join Classroom"**

**Note:** Classroom codes are case-insensitive and typically 6 characters long.

### Viewing Your Classrooms

1. Navigate to **Classrooms**
2. View all your enrolled classrooms including:
   - Classroom name and subject
   - Faculty name
   - Number of students enrolled
   - Number of exams available

### Viewing Classroom Details

Click on any classroom to view:
- Classroom information
- Faculty information
- Available exams
- Your submissions

### Leaving a Classroom

Currently, leaving a classroom requires faculty or admin intervention. Contact your teacher if you need to leave a classroom.

## Taking Exams

### Viewing Available Exams

1. Navigate to **Exams** from the dashboard
2. View all available exams including:
   - Exam title
   - Subject
   - Exam date
   - Duration
   - Status (Available, Completed)

### Exam Instructions

Before attempting an exam, review the instructions:

1. Click on an available exam
2. Read the exam instructions carefully
3. Note the exam duration
4. Review the total marks and pass percentage
5. Click **"Start Exam"** when ready

### Attempting an Exam

1. Navigate to **Exams**
2. Click on an available exam
3. Review instructions
4. Click **"Start Exam"**

**Important Notes:**
- The timer starts immediately when you click "Start Exam"
- You cannot pause the exam
- Your answers are auto-saved periodically
- Tab switching is monitored for integrity

### Navigating Questions

- Use the **"Previous"** and **"Next"** buttons to navigate
- Click on question numbers in the sidebar to jump to specific questions
- Marked questions are highlighted for easy reference

### Answering Questions

1. Read the question carefully
2. Select the correct option (A, B, C, or D)
3. Your answer is auto-saved
4. Navigate to the next question

### Submitting the Exam

1. Answer all questions
2. Review your answers if time permits
3. Click **"Submit Exam"**
4. Confirm the submission

**Note:** Once submitted, you cannot change your answers.

### Auto-Submit

If the timer runs out, the exam will be automatically submitted with your current answers.

### Exam Integrity

The system monitors:
- **Tab Switching:** Switching tabs during the exam is recorded
- **Time Taken:** Total time taken to complete the exam
- **Submission Time:** Timestamp of submission

Excessive tab switching may be flagged for review by faculty.

## Viewing Results

### Viewing Published Results

1. Navigate to **Results** from the dashboard
2. View all your results including:
   - Exam title
   - Score and percentage
   - Pass/Fail status
   - Date taken

### Viewing Detailed Results

1. Click on a result to view details
2. See:
   - Your answers
   - Correct answers
   - Score breakdown
   - Total marks
   - Pass percentage

### Result Availability

Results are only visible after:
1. You have submitted the exam
2. The faculty has published the results

If results are not published, you'll see a message indicating they are not yet available.

### Performance Validation

View your answer validation:
1. Click on a result
2. Click **"View Validation"**
3. See your answers compared to correct answers
4. Identify areas for improvement

## Profile Management

### Viewing Profile

1. Navigate to **Profile** from the top menu
2. View your account information:
   - Name and email
   - Registration number
   - Program and section
   - Profile picture
   - Account creation date

### Changing Password

1. Navigate to **Profile** → **Change Password**
2. Enter your current password
3. Enter your new password (minimum 6 characters)
4. Confirm your new password
5. Click **"Change Password"**

### Updating Profile Picture

1. Navigate to **Profile**
2. Click **"Upload Picture"**
3. Select an image file (JPG, JPEG, PNG)
4. Crop the image as desired
5. Click **"Save"**

**File Requirements:**
- Maximum size: 2MB
- Accepted formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG

### Removing Profile Picture

1. Navigate to **Profile**
2. Click **"Remove Picture"**
3. Confirm the removal

## Best Practices

### Exam Preparation

1. **Study Regularly:** Consistent study leads to better performance
2. **Review Instructions:** Read exam instructions carefully before starting
3. **Time Management:** Pace yourself according to the exam duration
4. **Technical Check:** Ensure your internet connection is stable
5. **Quiet Environment:** Take exams in a distraction-free environment

### During the Exam

1. **Read Questions Carefully:** Understand what each question is asking
2. **Manage Time:** Don't spend too long on difficult questions
3. **Review Answers:** If time permits, review your answers
4. **Stay Focused:** Avoid distractions and tab switching
5. **Submit Early:** Don't wait until the last second to submit

### After the Exam

1. **Review Results:** Analyze your performance when results are published
2. **Identify Weaknesses:** Focus on areas where you struggled
3. **Seek Feedback:** Ask teachers for clarification on difficult topics
4. **Track Progress:** Use analytics to monitor your improvement

### Technical Tips

1. **Browser Choice:** Use a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
2. **Internet Connection:** Ensure stable internet connection
3. **Device:** Use a computer or tablet for better experience
4. **Updates:** Keep your browser updated
5. **Backup:** Have a backup device ready if possible

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Cannot Join Classroom

- Verify the classroom code is correct
- Check if the classroom is archived
- Contact your teacher if the code doesn't work

#### Exam Not Available

- Verify the exam date has arrived
- Check if you're enrolled in the correct classroom
- Ensure the exam has been launched by faculty

#### Exam Timer Issues

- Refresh the page if timer freezes
- Contact your teacher immediately if issues persist
- Don't close the browser during the exam

#### Results Not Showing

- Verify results have been published by faculty
- Check if you've submitted the exam
- Contact your teacher if results are delayed

#### Login Problems

- Verify your email and password are correct
- Use the "Forgot Password" feature if needed
- Contact admin if account is locked

### Technical Issues

#### Page Not Loading

- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser

#### Images Not Loading

- Check your internet connection
- Disable ad blockers temporarily
- Ensure images are enabled in your browser

#### Slow Performance

- Close other browser tabs
- Close other applications
- Check your internet speed
- Try during off-peak hours

## Glossary

- **Classroom Code:** A unique code provided by teachers to join a classroom
- **Draft:** Exam status when being created (not visible to students)
- **Launched:** Exam status when available for students to attempt
- **Published:** Results status when visible to students
- **Tab Switching:** Switching between browser tabs during an exam (monitored)
- **Auto-save:** Automatic saving of answers during an exam
- **Pass Percentage:** Minimum percentage required to pass an exam
- **Analytics:** Statistical analysis of your performance
